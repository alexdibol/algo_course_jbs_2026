Algo Systems — Institutional Formation in Algorithmic Trading

A self-teaching architecture for algorithmic trading built to move from foundations to implementation with discipline, reproducibility, execution realism, and human accountability. The course is organized around three pillars that together define a coherent formation in modern trading systems.

Why This Structure

A Coherent Formation, Not a Loose Collection

The course is meant to function as a complete formation framework. It is not merely a repository of notes or isolated models. The three pillars are intended to work together so that technical method, strategic thought, and market understanding reinforce one another.
